Common Crochet Tools

To get started, you only need two things - Yarn and Hooks!

Yarn

Yarn comes in all shapes, sizes, types, and colors. When picking your yarn, you'll have to consider:

  • Weight- The thickness of the strand of the yarn.
  • Texture- Some yarns may look perfect for a sweater, but they might be super itchy and hard on your skin. Picking the yarn with the right texture will be important when planning projects.
  • Fiber Content- Is the yarn synthetic (Acrylic, Nylon), from an animal (Wool, Alpaca), or is it plant based (Cotton, Linen)? Is it a blend of these? These will affect how your project turns out.

In most cases, you won't have to guess which yarn works for which project. The labels on the yarn will list the recommended hooks and projects for you.

Hooks

Hooks come in different sizes to match the size of the yarn and the needs of your project. They're usually sized in millimeters(mm), ranging from 2 - 30 mm. Most projects that you will see will recommend sizes 4-8, so starting your toolkit with those would be best. However, many craft stores and online craft sites sell crochet kits with a wide range of hooks at an affordable cost!

Here are some characteristics to look for in a hook:

  • Material- What the hook is made of. Some materials may make working with certain yarns easier, while others are more ergonomic.
  • Hook Head- Inline Hooks may help you create more uniform stitches, while Tapered Hooks may make creating stitches easier.

Ultimately, which hooks you use and how you hold them is up to your personal preference. You'll discover your crochet style pretty quickly.

Recommended Projects

Below is a table of recommended types of projects based on the yarn and hooks you want to start with:

Yarn Type Yarn Characteristics Recommended Projects
Acrylic Soft, hypoallergenic, machine-washable, affordable, not breathable Fall & Winter clothes, decor
Cotton Soft, durable, absorbent, may lose shape when wet Dishclothes, home decor, summer clothes
Wool Warm, durable, can be scratchy based on the sheep Warm weather clothes and accessories
